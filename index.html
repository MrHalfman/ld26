Shrink Shift

Shrink Shift was made during the #26 LD.

Link to the entry.

Some words about the game...

Shrink shift was made with MelonJS (Thanks to Olivier Biot for this awesome framework). Feel free to discover MelonJS on melonjs.org! The theme of the game was Minimalist and we decided to make a Puzzle-Game. We made an entire new movement system to do so. Feel free to use the code or ask something, juste leave a comment.
