Shrink Shift

Goal

You are a removal man : the city where you live didn't have enough space. With your superpowers you have to clean & shrink flats by emptying them. Move items one by one to the exit, be careful : the world depends on you.

Keyboard Map

  • Z Q S D Or Arrows : Move Character
  • R : Retry level
  • Launch spells : Right click OR
    JumpOver : A
    E

Informations

Spells

To use a power : click on the power's button then push an arrow key in the direction you want to use de power.

Game elements

  • Blocking blocks : items can't be moved on these blocks.
  • Buttons & Door : Walking on a button opens the door of the same color.
  • Teleporters : Pushing an item on this item teleports this item to the same color teleporter.
