Help

Keyboard Map

  • Z Q S D Or Arrows : Move Character
  • R : Retry level
  • Click on a spell to launch it.

Help

Goal

You are a mover : the city where you live didn't have enough space. With your superpowers you have to clean & shrink flats by emptying them. Move items 1 by 1 to the exit, be careful : the world is counting on you.

Spells

To use a power : click on the power's button then push an arrow key in the direction you want to use de power.

Game elements

  • Blocking blocks : items can't be moved on these blocks.
  • Buttons & Door : Walking on a button opens the door of the same color.
  • Teleporters : Pushing an item or walking on it teleports to the same color teleporter.
